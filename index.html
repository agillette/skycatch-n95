About

N95.supplies started with the need to support our own Skycatch employees and give them access to much needed N95 masks quickly during their deployments.

The team has been actively involved in helping local private institutions get access to masks, we quickly discovered how hard it was to find the right sources.

The Skycatch has decades of experience sourcing high quality products parts in China and several strong partners who are able to support us on the ground with screening quality, capacity, and reliability of manufacturers.

Through this process and thanks to our vast connections in China we were able to connect directly with the largest manufacturers of masks in Mainland China to reduce lead time and middleman markup.

Due to the intense demand, we will prioritize supply to serve the needs of medical professionals and charitable efforts.
